How to use

  1. Enter the amount and choose the GST rate.
  2. Pick whether your amount includes GST or excludes GST.
  3. See the tax amount and final total instantly.

Frequently asked questions

Which GST rates are supported?

The calculator supports common rates like 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%, plus a custom rate option.

Can I calculate reverse GST?

Yes. Inclusive mode extracts GST from a tax-inclusive total.
